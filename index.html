ℹ️ About MapMyWeather

MapMyWeather is an interactive weather map showing real-time conditions for cities worldwide. Features include:

  • 🌡️ °C/°F temperature toggle
  • 📊 12-hour hourly forecasts
  • 🌧️ Rain predictions and alerts
  • 🧠 Smart weather insights and recommendations
  • 🗺️ Interactive map with click-to-weather
  • ⭐ Save and manage favorite cities
  • 📍 Auto-detect your location
  • 📈 Temperature and rain trend charts
  • 🎮 Play WeatherGuessr - Test your weather knowledge!
